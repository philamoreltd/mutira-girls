Computer as a subject has been able to teach students how to navigate the everchanging world of technology by offering innovative Computer lessons

Business Studies

Business Studies subject seeks to produce learners who are all rounded and ready to pursue various business related courses in Business management and Accounting at Higher Education level. We have been having tremendous improvement in Business Studies subject in the previous years. It has helped majority of the students transit to university level.

Agriculture

Agriculture is not only taught theoretically but also practically using various techniques such as demonstration in the school farm, playing animations related to the subject, using the smart screen and exposure of learners to the compound. The school has a number of farm structures within the school compound. The school has a number of farm enterprises where students are given opportunities to visit and observe various practices carried out there. These enterprises include dairy farming, banana plantation, pig industry, agroforestry and horticultural farming.

Art & Design

'Design' refers to planning, coming up with ideas and putting in measures so that ideas become reality. Design includes architecture, engineering and production. It's a broad field and all creative work depends on a good design process.
According to the national curriculum, a high-quality art and design education should engage, inspire and challenge pupils, equipping them with the knowledge and skills to experiment, invent and create their own works of art, craft and design.

Music

This is a mistake, with schools losing not only an enjoyable subject, but a subject that can enrich students’ lives and education.
